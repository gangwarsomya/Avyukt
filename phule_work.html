Literary Works of Savitribai Phule

Savitribai Phule also wrote poetry and prose. She also published a poem titled "Go, Get Education" in which she urged those who are oppressed to free themselves by getting an education. She published Kavya Phule in 1854 and Bavan Kashi Subodh Ratnakar in 1892. She developed into a fervent feminist as a result of her experiences and work. To promote awareness of issues relating to women's rights, she founded the Mahila Seva Mandal. She also demanded that there be a place where women could congregate that was devoid of any form of caste-based prejudice. The requirement that every woman in attendance sit on the same mat served as a symbol of this. She advocated against infanticide as well.

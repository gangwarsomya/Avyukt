Women In Indian Literature

A canon that almost completely ignored women's writing more than 200 years ago has dominated a large portion of the world's literature. Although the counter-canons that have emerged as a result of this exclusion have helped to mainstream women's writing, they still in some ways fail to acknowledge women's literature from non-white countries. The goal of this project is to showcase some of the historical works created in India by women. To explore more about a writer, click on their name heading.
